Company Overview

At Insto Cosmetics Pvt Ltd we have devoted more than 24 years to establish as an internationally recognized expert in the research, development and production of technologically advanced cosmetics products.

"Where art blends with science, creativity is a passion, projects become personal vision and inspired choices in development leading to outstanding results".

Who we are?

We are one of the leading cosmetic manufacturers and exporters established in 1994 and located in Nashik, India.

Insto Cosmetics Private Limited is ISO 22716:2007 certified company and we follow all GMP standards and guidelines.

Our team consists of innovative scientists, skilled workers and experienced executives.

We provide assistance every step of the way and deliver the highest quality of service to ensure your product is of the highest marketable and consumable standard.

What we do?

We offer a number of services under one roof, designed to satisfy your cosmetic project requirements whether small or large volume.

Research & Development - Our department comprises group of top notch professionals including chemists and scientists. They consistently research on upcoming trends and work on improvements to meet all the quality standards.

Export - We provide a total researched production solution to our abroad clients and export them with best quality products which are highly price efficient and competitive in international markets. We are ready for developing and exporting any kind of cosmetics products under private label or contract manufacturing.

Private Labelling - With an extensive portfolio of superior products, Insto Cosmetics provides opportunities for private label branding and also specializing in custom formulation and contract manufacturing.

How we do?

Selection of Raw Material - We concentrate on continuous research and analysis of the latest trends & developments in health and beauty industry.We study for premium raw materials and select the best suitable material.

Industrial Mixers - We have high quality mixing vessel machine which results to give quality results.

Manufacturing & Quality Control - All products are produced and filled in controlled Clean Manufacturing Areas and are operated to the Total Quality Management System principles, providing full traceability.

Branding and Packaging - This is a vital element in the development of your private label product. Our experienced team will work closely with you to create the best packaging that your products deserve.

Work is our Passion

Our Perception - To reach to a great level where everyone has access to high quality cosmetic products.

Our Mission - To deliver quality of service that ensures your product is of the highest marketable and consumable standard.

Our Values - Time is money. Work towards customer satisfaction.
